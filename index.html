03 — context

Why this is the foundation, not the feature

The entire BoB vision — smarter lists, better targeting, relationship continuity — rests on one thing: structured data from every call.

What the program already envisions
  • Intent-based lists via OCTO (who to call, what to offer, when)
  • Pre-call brief surfaced automatically at connect
  • Relationship continuity across agents
  • Next-best-action from Fuel iX during live calls
Why it stalls without this solution
  • OCTO cannot build intent-based lists without structured input data
  • Pre-call briefs are empty if nothing was captured from prior calls
  • Relationship continuity requires the CRM to hold the relationship
  • Fuel iX recommendations are only as good as the context fed in

Our in-flight solutions 01

Turning conversations into usable data

A strategic assessment of how TELUS CSS outbound can move from transactional cold-calling to relationship-based, intelligence-driven selling.

What the program already envisions
  • Intent-based lists via OCTO (who to call, what to offer, when)
  • Pre-call brief surfaced automatically at connect
  • Relationship continuity across agents
  • Next-best-action from Fuel iX during live calls
The solution

Turn conversations into usable data

The operation runs four million outbound calls a month, yet almost nothing customers say survives the call. Capture it — call by call — as structured data the business can act on, and everything downstream gets sharper: richer profiles, better targeting, context for every future agent.

The current state · today

Every call disappears into a recording nobody uses

Every call contains rich customer context — but today nearly all of it is lost the moment the call ends. Forty-five days later, a different agent calls the same person with no context. The customer remembers. The agent doesn't.

What a customer actually says
I'm with Bell, 6 months left on contract
Price is a bit high for me right now
Don't call me before noon, I work nights
My wife might be interested in home internet
I love watching sports, especially hockey
What gets recorded in the system
no sale
call back later
 
 
 

"Right now our disposition codes suck... sale, no sale, call back later, not interested. It doesn't tell me anything about the customer."

— Edward Zhao, Director Global Sales Operations
Why it stalls without this solution
  • OCTO cannot build intent-based lists without structured input data
  • Pre-call briefs are empty if nothing was captured from prior calls
  • Relationship continuity requires the CRM to hold the relationship
  • Fuel iX recommendations are only as good as the context fed in

The solution, working

The co-pilot reads the transcript and suggests structured fields

The same call, captured. Fuel iX analyses the transcript and surfaces field suggestions — the agent confirms in one tap. The AI suggests, the human verifies.
